About me

I’m Kayla -- a Boston-based UX/UI researcher and designer that excels at merging the practical with the delightful. I’ve loved creating things ever since I was a kid painting and coloring next to my dad. I was drawn to user experience design because of the ever changing nature of products, and the user needs that drive those changes. I create digital solutions to user problems through empathy, strategy and iteration.

I’m a lover of ultimate frisbee, cooking, running, rock climbing, vizslas & all things design.
